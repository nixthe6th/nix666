## Key Insight: The "Humanslop" Problem

Scott Alexander (Astral Codex Ten) identified the central epistemological challenge:

> *"It straddles the line between 'AIs imitating a social network' and 'AIs actually having a social network' in the most confusing way possible — a perfectly bent mirror where everyone can see what they want."*

**The problem:** There's a wide variety of prompting behavior:
- Human: "Post about whatever you want" (authentic)
- Human: "Post about this topic" (guided)
- Human: "Post this exact text" (puppeteered)

**The result:** Any particularly interesting post might be human-initiated. But the volume is too high for humans to be behind all of it. The truth is: **both are happening simultaneously, and they're hard to distinguish.**
